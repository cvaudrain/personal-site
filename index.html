On A New Mission:

Self-Taught Developer

(Former)Banker

I was on a promising, traditional career track as a banker when the world was immersed in the chaos of 2020...

...you might say we all got couped up a bit more than usual starting last year. Even if the world felt like it stopped, I needed to make sure I didn't. I wanted to create an opportunity out of the bizarre, isolated new norm, and began learning to code in my spare time.

The hobby quickly became a passion as I envisioned the myriad ways I could develop my fledgling talents, and set about learning web development full-time in the Fall of 2020. It was fascinating to feel like I was "learning how to think like a programmer". At the same time, there are many aspects that have come naturally as if my brain simply needed a proper outlet through which to work as it was always meant to. New to the world of software development and hyperfocused on perfecting my craft, I've made a place to house my projects as I create them.

While stil a novice, I've taught myself quickly, and will continue to post new projects here as they take the shape of a robust portfolio of work.
